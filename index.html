Tips From Satimu

Here are some tips from satimu

Using Photos

People, especially prospective clients would like to see more of your work and facilities through photos. Therefore, engage your clients in more visual platforms and it will certainly yield great results.

Prices And Rates

Reduce on the number of times you mention prices and rates for your business. This chases clients away. Rather, let them ask you about your rates instead.

Startup Businesses

If you really want to start a business, you need to address the reasons you think you can't start a business and get rid of them. Find a solution to the issue rather than let it hold you back.

Listen

Listen to what others have to say—friends, family, experts, even yourself. When it comes to things that have to do with your entrepreneurial goals, be a sponge.

Be A Solution

Rather than starting your idea with what to sell, think about what it will solve. It's a lot easier to gain a soild customer base when your business is fixing a problem.

Keep It Simple

If you're like many entreprenueurs, you have a business idea and you're ready to run with it. Be careful not to let your concept snowball into something overcomplicated.
